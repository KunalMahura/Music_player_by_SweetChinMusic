Favourite Singers

Mohammed Rafi

A legendary playback singer known for his versatility and timeless melodies.

Mohammed Rafi is regarded as one of the greatest playback singers in the history of Indian cinema. His career spanned over three decades, during which he lent his voice to countless iconic songs in various genres. From romantic ballads like "Chaudhvin Ka Chand" to patriotic anthems like "Kar Chale Hum Fida," Rafi's golden voice continues to inspire millions around the world.

Sidhu Moosewala

A legendary figure in Punjabi music known for his lyrical genius.

Sidhu Moosewala revolutionized Punjabi music with his hard-hitting lyrics and unique style. His songs like "295," "So High," and "Same Beef" have garnered millions of fans worldwide, cementing his legacy as one of the greatest of all time.

Karan Aujla

A rising star known for his lyrical depth and powerful vocals.

Karan Aujla is known for his thought-provoking lyrics and captivating music videos. Tracks like "Don't Look," "Chitta Kurta," and "Haan Haige Aa" showcase his immense talent and growing influence in the industry.
